Meet Veroanica

The Person Behind
the Practice.

I started this work because I kept meeting people who were highly capable but quietly struggling: executives burning out, students losing confidence, teams that had stopped trusting each other.

Psychology gave me the language. Coaching gave me the tools. And 10+ years of working with real people in real situations gave me the one thing no degree can teach: knowing when to listen, and when to challenge.

I hold a Master's in Psychology with advanced training in counselling and guidance, but what I bring to every session isn't just a framework. It's genuine curiosity about what's holding you back, and a real commitment to helping you move forward.

About Cognita Mindcare

Founded on One Belief.

Cognita Mindcare was founded on a single belief: psychological well-being is the foundation of both personal fulfilment and organisational success. We blend evidence-based psychology with practical, human-centred coaching, working with individuals and organisations across the globe, entirely remotely, to create change that lasts.

Over 10 years of practice, 200+ sessions conducted across the globe.
