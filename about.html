About Me

Meet Veroanika

Veroanika is a Corporate Psychologist and Lifestyle Mentor focused on human behaviour, emotional well-being, and meaningful psychological growth. She works with individuals and organisations to move beyond surface-level functioning — into clarity, awareness, and lasting change.

With experience across IT organisations and educational institutions, she brings a grounded understanding of individual psychology and group dynamics, bridging academic rigour with real-world impact.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long is a typical coaching engagement?

Most individual coaching packages run 8–12 sessions over 3–6 months. We also offer single sessions and intensive 1-day formats.

Do you offer virtual sessions?

Yes — all individual coaching and most workshops are available virtually via Zoom, making it accessible regardless of your location.

Is coaching confidential?

Absolutely. All sessions are strictly confidential. The only exceptions are standard duty-of-care situations, which we discuss openly at the outset.

How is coaching different from therapy?

Coaching focuses on your present goals and future potential. Therapy addresses past trauma and mental health conditions. Many clients benefit from both.
