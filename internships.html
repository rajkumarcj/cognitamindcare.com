Our internship programs are designed for individuals who want to move beyond theoretical learning and truly understand psychology in practice. Whether you are a psychology student or a working professional, this program provides a structured space to observe, reflect, and apply psychological concepts in real-life contexts.

We currently offer:

  • •  Counselling Psychology Internship — for students
  • •  Organisational Psychology Internship — for working professionals

This is not just a course — it is an immersive learning experience designed to build clarity, confidence, and professional readiness.

Who Is This For?

Two dedicated tracks designed for where you are in your journey.

🎓
Students — Counselling Psychology

Build Your Foundations

  • Build strong counselling foundations through real-world exposure
  • Understand real-life case applications beyond the classroom
  • Develop your therapeutic communication and listening skills
  • Work alongside an experienced practitioner as your mentor
💼
Working Professionals — Organisational Psychology

Apply Psychology at Work

  • Apply psychology principles in real workplace settings
  • Understand employee behaviour, motivation, and group dynamics
  • Support workplace well-being and productivity initiatives
  • Gain structured corporate psychology experience

What You Will Learn

A comprehensive curriculum that bridges academic knowledge with real-world practice.

Counselling Skills & Techniques

Core therapeutic approaches, active listening, and evidence-based intervention strategies.

Cognitive Understanding & Behaviour

Explore how thoughts, emotions, and behaviours interconnect in clinical and everyday contexts.

Ethics & Professional Boundaries

Navigate complex ethical situations with confidence, integrity, and professional standards.

Real-Life Case Analysis

Case-based learning after every session — analyse, discuss, and draw practical insights.

Psychological Application — Personal

Apply psychology to everyday life situations including stress, identity, relationships, and growth.

Psychological Application — Corporate

Understand workplace dynamics, leadership psychology, and organisational well-being strategies.
